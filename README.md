## The Problem

Remote teams waste time converting meeting times across time zones. "Is 2 PM EST good for you?" requires mental math and timezone lookups.

## The Solution

Meeting Time Finder lets you:
- Select a time once
- Add multiple time zones
- See all times instantly
- Identify business hours at a glance
- Copy all times to share with your team

## Usage

1. **Select a time** using the time picker (or click "Now" for current time)
2. **Add time zones** from the dropdown menu
3. **View all times** - they update automatically
4. **Copy to clipboard** to share with your team
5. **Remove zones** you don't need anymore
